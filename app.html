Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Royal Bites app free to use?

Yes. The app is completely free to download and use for placing orders, accessing rewards, and viewing promotions.

Do I need an account to use the app?

You may browse the menu freely, but an account is required to place orders, collect rewards, and redeem coupons.

Can I track my order through the app?

Yes. Real-time order tracking is available for in-app delivery and takeaway orders.
