Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your personal data at Royal Bites.

1. Why This Matters

At Royal Bites, your privacy is a top priority. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect it.

2. What We Collect

  • Contact details (name, email, phone)
  • Order and delivery info
  • Payment and billing details
  • Browser/device data and activity

3. How We Use Your Info

We use your information to fulfill orders, improve services, personalize offers, provide customer support, and ensure security.

4. Who We Share It With

Only trusted partners like payment gateways, delivery teams, and support services—under strict confidentiality agreements. Never sold. Never abused.

5. Your Control

You can access, update, or delete your data anytime by contacting us. You can also opt-out of marketing emails at any time.

6. Security Practices

We use encrypted systems, secure servers, and access restrictions to ensure your data stays private and protected.

7. Cookies & Tracking

Cookies help us offer you a better user experience. You can disable them in your browser, but some features may not work optimally.

8. Children’s Information

Our platform is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Updates to This Policy

This policy may change occasionally. Updates will be posted here with a revised effective date. We recommend reviewing it periodically.

10. Questions?

Reach us at our Contact Page if you have any concerns about your privacy or how we handle your information.
