Careers at Royal Bites

Build your future with Malaysia's leading fast casual dining brand.

Why Join Royal Bites?

Career Advancement

We provide structured training, clear pathways for promotion, and internal mobility across our nationwide network.

Flexible Work Opportunities

Positions available across various departments and branches, with part-time and full-time options to suit your goals.

Comprehensive Staff Benefits

Staff meals, employee discounts, performance incentives, and annual bonus schemes included.

Nationwide Coverage

With over 200 branches across 14 states, you can grow your career close to home or take on new challenges in other regions.

Current Career Opportunities

Restaurant Manager

Location: Nationwide

Salary: RM4,000 – RM6,500 / month

Lead daily operations, manage staff performance, ensure food quality and service excellence while driving customer satisfaction and profitability.

  • Minimum 3 years’ experience in F&B management
  • Strong leadership and communication skills
  • Knowledge of safety & hygiene standards
Apply Now

Restaurant Crew Member

Location: All Outlets

Salary: RM1,800 – RM2,500 / month

Provide friendly customer service, take orders, prepare food and beverages, and maintain cleanliness of dining and kitchen areas.

  • Energetic and team-oriented
  • Fast learner, no experience required
  • Flexible hours, training provided
Apply Now

Delivery Rider

Location: Urban Areas

Salary: RM2,000 – RM3,000 / month + incentives

Deliver meals quickly and safely using GPS, ensure food quality, and provide top-notch customer interaction at the doorstep.

  • Own motorcycle & valid license
  • Attractive delivery incentives
  • Mobile app navigation support
Apply Now

Marketing Executive

Plan campaigns, analyze market trends, grow brand awareness, and support digital promotions for new launches and seasonal offers.

  • Degree in Marketing or related field
  • Proficient in social media tools
  • Creative and data-driven
Apply Now

Human Resource Executive

Location: HQ – George Town

Salary: RM3,200 – RM5,000 / month

Manage recruitment, onboarding, employee relations, and support a positive work culture across all outlets.

  • Strong understanding of HR policies
  • People-oriented and organized
  • Experience in hospitality industry a plus

IT Support Technician

Location: HQ – George Town

Salary: RM3,000 – RM4,800 / month

Provide technical assistance across the company, troubleshoot hardware/software issues, and maintain IT systems for seamless operations across all outlets.
