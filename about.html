About Royal Bites

From humble beginnings to a household name in Malaysia’s fast food scene.

Haziq Danial, Founder of Royal Bites (est. 1965)

Our Story

Royal Bites was born in 1965, founded by the passionate and visionary Haziq Danial — a true pioneer of flavor. It began humbly at a tiny roadside stall just outside Permatang Pauh, Penang, where he grilled his now-legendary Mega Royal Beef burgers under the tropical sky.

Inspired by a growing love for Western dining trends and the unique essence of Malaysian street food culture, Haziq crafted a bold fusion of East and West. His burgers were not just food — they were an experience.

From sizzling patties on a charcoal grill to the smiles of loyal customers lining up night after night, Royal Bites quickly grew from a family passion to a national sensation. Affordability, consistency, and flavor-packed quality became our foundation.

Today, Royal Bites stands tall as more than a burger chain — it's a legacy. With every meal we serve, we honour Haziq’s original dream: food that brings people together.

What began as a humble burger stand on the coast of Penang has grown exponentially into a thriving, multi-million ringgit business. From one grill to over 200 locations nationwide, our journey has been nothing short of extraordinary.
