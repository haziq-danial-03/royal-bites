Frequently Asked Questions

What are Royal Bites' operating hours?

Most of our Royal Bites outlets are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including weekends and public holidays. This ensures that you can enjoy our meals at any time of the day or night. However, outlets located within shopping malls or specific commercial zones may operate according to the venue’s business hours. We recommend checking with the specific branch via our Restaurant Locator for the most accurate timing.

How do I place an order online?

Ordering online is quick and convenient. Simply visit our Order Page or download the Royal Bites app from the App Store or Google Play. Once installed, sign up or log in, choose your preferred outlet, browse the menu, customize your order, and check out. We offer real-time order tracking and multiple payment options including credit/debit cards, e-wallets, and cash-on-delivery for eligible areas.

Does Royal Bites offer delivery services?

Yes, we proudly offer 24/7 delivery services for your convenience. Delivery is available within a 50km radius of most participating outlets. You can place your order through our mobile app or website and choose between standard, express, or scheduled delivery. All our delivery personnel are trained to handle food safely, and you can track your rider in real-time. We also partner with platforms like Foodpanda and GrabFood in selected areas.

Are there any vegetarian or allergen-free options?

Currently, Royal Bites does not offer designated vegetarian or allergen-free menu items. Our core offerings focus on meat-based meals, and we operate in shared kitchen environments where cross-contact with allergens is possible. While we strive to maintain high food safety standards, we are unable to guarantee the absence of common allergens such as dairy, gluten, or nuts. Customers with dietary restrictions or allergies are encouraged to consult our staff for more information prior to ordering.

How can I apply for a job at Royal Bites?

You can explore career opportunities by visiting our Careers page. There, you’ll find a list of current openings across all departments including restaurant staff, delivery operations, corporate roles, and internships. The page includes detailed job descriptions and requirements. Applications can be submitted directly via our online form, where you can upload your resume and specify your preferred location. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted via email or phone for interviews.

Do you have a loyalty or rewards program?

Absolutely. Royal Bites Rewards is our official loyalty program designed to give back to our valued customers. You can explore our programs when you Download App. Once registered through our app, every purchase earns you points which can be redeemed for free meals, discounts, and exclusive merchandise. You’ll also gain access to early-bird promotions, birthday gifts, and member-only events. The more you order, the more you earn. Points do not expire as long as you make a purchase within a rolling 6-month window.

Is your food certified Halal?

Yes, all Royal Bites products are 100% certified Halal by the Department of Islamic Development Malaysia (JAKIM). We strictly follow Halal practices in sourcing, preparation, and handling processes at every stage of our operations. Our kitchens are regularly audited to ensure compliance with Halal standards. Customers can dine with confidence knowing that all meals served are prepared in accordance with Islamic dietary laws.

Can I host a private party or event at Royal Bites?

Yes, we offer event hosting at selected flagship outlets. Whether it’s a birthday party, corporate lunch, or community gathering, our event packages can be tailored to suit your needs. We provide private seating areas, custom menus, themed décor, and dedicated staff to assist throughout the event. Advance booking is required, and additional charges may apply depending on the package selected. Please contact your preferred outlet for further information.

How can I give feedback or file a complaint?

Your feedback matters. If you have any compliments, concerns, or complaints, you can reach out to us via our Contact Us page. Alternatively, you can email us at support@royalbites.com or call our customer service hotline at 1-800-ROYAL-88. We strive to respond to all queries within 48 hours and are committed to continuous improvement based on customer experiences.

Where can I find nutritional information about your menu items?

Nutritional details including calorie count, macronutrient breakdown, and allergen information are available on our Menu page and through the Royal Bites app. You may also request printed copies in-store. We aim to provide full transparency so that customers can make informed choices, especially those with dietary goals or health conditions. All data is verified and updated quarterly by certified nutrition consultants.
